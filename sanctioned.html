Sanctioned Tournament

Lancers is proud to be an active participant in the YGO Cebu Community Sanctioned Tournaments. While our own tournaments at Lancers HQ offer a great way to enjoy the game, we also take part in these official events organized by the broader YGO Cebu Community.

These Sanctioned Tournaments are where serious duelists come to compete at a higher level, with official recognition and prizes on the line. By joining forces with the YGO Cebu Community, we give our members the opportunity to test their skills against the best in Cebu and beyond.
