Lancers HQ


Welcome to Lancers HQ! We are a passionate small business based in Cebu, dedicated to bringing the excitement of Yu-Gi-Oh! to our community. Located near the University of Cebu, our main headquarters is where our love for the game comes to life.


At Lancers HQ, we strive to foster a supportive and engaging community through tutorials, discussions, gameplay showcases, and live duels. Our goal is to share the thrill of the game, one duel at a time, while inspiring others to join the fun. We're passionate about Yu-Gi-Oh! and committed to helping others develop their skills, form friendships, and enjoy this incredible hobby to its fullest.


Join us at Lancers, where the heart of Yu-Gi-Oh! beats strong. Let's duel!

Mission

At Lancers HQ, our mission is to share our beloved hobby with others and invite new players into the exciting world of Yu-Gi-Oh! We are dedicated to creating an inviting and welcoming community where players of all skill levels can feel at home.

We strive to play with fellow duelists, fostering friendships and encouraging the growth of the community. By sharing strategies, hosting duels, and providing support to players both new and experienced, we aim to help every duelist enhance their skills and passion for the game. Our goal is not only to grow as players ourselves but also to contribute to the overall growth and spirit of the Yu-Gi-Oh! community.

Vision

At Lancers HQ, our vision is to create a large and vibrant community where duelists from all walks of life can come together to share their love for Yu-Gi-Oh! and engage in friendly competition. We aspire to become a hub for passionate players, where camaraderie, skill-building, and a shared love for the game flourish.

Our long-term goal is to grow into a thriving community that not only connects duelists locally but also competes at the national, regional, and even international levels. We aim to nurture talent, promote healthy competition, and establish a presence in major Yu-Gi-Oh! events, all while maintaining the inclusive and welcoming spirit of Lancers HQ.
